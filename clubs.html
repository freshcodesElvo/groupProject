OUR CLUBS

The JKUAT HUB CLUB serves as the umbrella for a diverse range of specialized clubs, each
designed to cater to specific interests and foster focused development. While each club has its
unique focus, they all share our core values of collaboration, learning, and impact.

Here are some of the exciting clubs under our umbrella:
Chess Club

Beyond the board, chess is a powerful tool for developing the mind. We are a community of passionate players dedicated to sharpening our strategic thinking, enhancing our problem-solving skills, and fostering an environment of intellectual growth. Join us for weekly tournaments where you can test your mettle against fellow students and friendly matches that offer a relaxed setting for practice and improvement. Whether you're a complete beginner eager to learn the basics, an intermediate player looking to refine your opening game, or a seasoned master seeking a worthy opponent, our club offers a welcoming and inclusive space for all. We believe that every move is a lesson, and every game is an opportunity to grow. Come join us, strategize your way to victory, and unlock your full intellectual potential.
