Be A Force For Good

The JKUAT Red Cross Chapter is a beacon of hope and service, dedicated to saving lives and building resilient communities on campus and beyond.

JKUAT RED CROSS CHAPTER

As a proud chapter of the Kenya Red Cross Society, we are driven by the fundamental principles of humanity, impartiality, neutrality, independence, voluntary service, unity, and universality. We empower students to be proactive agents of change, ready to respond to emergencies, promote health and safety, and serve those in need.

Our Core Activities

  • First Aid and Disaster Response
  • Community Health & Sanitation
  • Blood Donation Drives
  • Peer Education & Advocacy

More Than a Club: A Lifeline of Service

The JKUAT Red Cross Chapter is a place where compassion meets action. We provide comprehensive training and hands-on experience that not only prepares you for real-world emergencies but also instills values of empathy and leadership.

Our members are trained to be:

  • Certified First Aid Responders
  • Disaster Preparedness Leaders
  • Community Health Advocates

Joining us means gaining skills that can save a life and becoming part of a global network dedicated to humanitarian service. You'll build a legacy of kindness that extends far beyond the university campus.

Why Join the JKUAT Red Cross Chapter?

  • Impactful Community Service:

    Participate in life-saving initiatives, from campus-wide blood donation drives to assisting in local community health campaigns. Your work directly benefits others.

  • Professional Training & Certification:

    Get certified in first aid and other vital skills, giving you an invaluable advantage for future careers in healthcare, public service, and humanitarian aid.

  • Leadership Development:

    Take on leadership roles and learn how to organize, manage, and execute projects that make a tangible difference in people's lives.

  • A Global Network:

    As part of the Kenya Red Cross Society, you'll connect with a vast network of volunteers and professionals, opening doors to opportunities both locally and internationally.

Our Impact in Action

Our commitment to service is evident in the positive changes we bring to our community. Here are some of our recent activities and achievements:

  • Campus-Wide First Aid Training: We successfully trained over 200 students and staff in basic first aid, creating a safer environment for everyone on campus.

  • Community Health Outreach: Our teams conducted a successful medical camp in a nearby community, providing essential health checks and hygiene education.

  • Record-Breaking Blood Drives: We organized a series of blood donation campaigns that led to a significant increase in contributions to local blood banks, helping save countless lives.

How to Get Involved

Ready to answer the call of service? Joining our chapter is the first step towards making a difference:

  • Where We Meet: Student Center, Room C, or the JKUAT Red Cross Office
  • When We Meet: Every Tuesday from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM
  • Stay Connected: Follow our social media pages for updates on events and emergency response calls!

Join us and start your journey of service and compassion!
