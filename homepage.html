Clubs Highlights

Robotics Team Wins

Our dedicated Robotics Club showcased their incredible talent and hard work, clinching the top spot in the highly competitive national inter-university robotics competition. Their innovative design and flawless execution set them apart from the competition, bringing home a prestigious trophy for the university.

Coding Bootcamp Success

The Linux Club successfully hosted a week-long coding bootcamp, empowering over 50 students with foundational programming skills in Python and Bash scripting. The event was met with enthusiastic participation and helped foster a new generation of developers within the university.

Web3 Hackathon

The Web3 Club organized its first-ever hackathon, bringing together developers and innovators to build decentralized applications. The event fostered a vibrant community, with participants creating a range of projects from new blockchain protocols to secure digital wallets.
