Why Join the JKUAT Chess Club?

  • Improve Your Game:

    Our club offers a welcoming environment for all skill levels. Learn fundamental strategies, explore advanced tactics, and get personalized tips from more experienced players.

  • Weekly Tournaments:

    Put your skills to the test in our friendly but competitive weekly tournaments. Compete for bragging rights, track your progress, and climb the club's leaderboard.

  • Community and Networking:

    Connect with a diverse group of students from various fields of study. Chess is a universal language, and our club provides a unique opportunity to build lasting friendships and expand your network.

  • Represent JKUAT:

    As a proud affiliate of the JKUAT HUB CLUB, we represent the university in local, national, and even regional competitions. Join us and help carry on our legacy of excellence in the East African games and other chess leagues.
